Computer Buyback Program

We offer competitive prices for your used computers based on their condition and market value. Our evaluation process is transparent and fair.

How We Evaluate Your Computer

  • Age and specifications of the device
  • Physical condition
  • Functionality of all components
  • Current market value
  • Demand for the specific model

Condition Guidelines

Condition Description Value Range
Like New No visible wear, all components perfect, original packaging 70-80% of retail value
Excellent Minor wear, fully functional, no repairs needed 60-70% of retail value
Good Normal wear, fully functional, minor cosmetic issues 40-60% of retail value
Fair Significant wear, functional, may need minor repairs 20-40% of retail value
Poor Heavy wear, requires repairs, missing components 10-20% of retail value

Buyback Process

  1. Submit your computer's details through our online form
  2. Receive an initial estimate within 24 hours
  3. Bring your computer for in-person evaluation
  4. Receive final offer and payment on the spot
